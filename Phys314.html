Phys314 — Quantum Mechanics II


Purpose of this course

In this second semester of Qunatum Mechanics we will broaden our understanding of the theory, considering techniques that can lead us to approximate solutions of more complicated systems. Application of quantum formalism to descriptions of atomic systems, solid state systems, nuclei and other subatomic particles will be presented.
