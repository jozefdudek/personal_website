Phys357 — Careers in Physics


Purpose of this course

A set of invited speakers from various industries will discuss how having a degree in physics has benefitted them in their careers.

Class Schedule

Fridays, 2pm — 2.50pm in Small Hall Room 111.

Policies

You are expected to attend as many of the class sessions as possible. There is no mandatory out-of-class work, and no exams, but you should consider investigating online the industry of the next speaker in order to better focus your questions for them. The class will be graded pass/fail.
